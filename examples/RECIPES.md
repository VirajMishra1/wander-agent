# 🍳 Wander Agent Recipes

Copy-paste these straight to your AI (Claude Desktop, Cursor, Windsurf — anything with MCP). No setup beyond `pip install wander-agent`. The AI picks the right tools automatically.

---

## 🛫 Find fares the airlines hide

> *"Find me a flight from New York to Tokyo in August the airlines don't want me to see."*

Triggers hidden-city and split-ticket search — two separate tickets routed through a hub, which online travel agencies are contractually banned from showing you. Often 30–50% cheaper.

> *"What's the cheapest day to fly from London to Bangkok next month?"*

Day-of-week + fare-calendar scan across the whole month.

> *"Scan for mistake fares and error pricing right now."*

Pulls live deal-alert feeds. Prices expire fast.

---

## 🌴 Plan a whole trip in one sentence

> *"Plan a 10-day trip to Bali for 2 people leaving from Dubai in September, budget $3,000."*

One call returns flights + hotels + visa + weather + advisory + restaurants + packing list + day-by-day itinerary.

> *"I have 4 days in Tokyo in October. Build me an itinerary with weather and local events for each day."*

---

## 🛂 Passports & visas

> *"Which of my two passports — Indian and Portuguese — gets me into more countries visa-free?"*

Dual-passport diff with Henley index rankings and the exact destinations each unlocks.

> *"Do I need a transit visa to connect through Doha on a US passport?"*

> *"List every country I can enter visa-free on a UK passport."*

---

## 💳 Points & miles

> *"Turn my Amex Membership Rewards points into the best business-class flight to Europe."*

Sweet-spot finder across transfer partners.

> *"Should I pay cash or use points for this $600 flight? I have 50,000 Chase points."*

---

## 🌍 Where should I even go?

> *"I have $1,500 and a week off in November. Where can I fly to from JFK that's warm and safe?"*

Composes cheap-anywhere search + destination scoring (cost + weather + safety + events) + visa checks.

> *"Show me the best aurora-viewing destinations next week with the KP-index forecast."*

---

## ⚠️ Is it safe / smart to go?

> *"Is it safe to travel to [country] right now? Give me the advisory level, weather, and any recent travel news."*

Live US State Dept advisory + 16-day forecast + recent strikes/closures/entry bans.
